Anna — Reiki & Energy Healing

Anna is the heart of the healing work at the Farm. A Reiki Master with deep roots in energy medicine, she brings an intuitive and gentle approach to each session — creating a space where whatever needs to surface, can. Her work with the horses is particularly extraordinary; the combination of Reiki and equine presence opens something in people that's hard to put into words.

She offers sessions both privately and within the herd paddock, and facilitates the Private Equine Therapy + Yin + Reiki immersions alongside our equine therapy practitioners.

  • Reiki — private sessions, in the treatment room or outdoors
  • Reiki with the Herd — energy healing in the paddock
  • Private Equine Assisted Therapy + Yin with Reiki
