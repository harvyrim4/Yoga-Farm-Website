Our Story

A Place to Arrive

Yoga Farm is 32 acres where you can actually slow down — not just talk about it. Most people say they feel it as soon as they arrive, before a class has even started or a horse has come over to say hello. We've brought together yoga, equine therapy, retreats, and a small community of practitioners, because healing rarely looks like just one thing.

Some people come for an hour, some for a weekend, some keep coming back for years. However you find your way here, you're welcome exactly as you are.
