About This Work

The Horses Hold Space

Horses are extraordinarily attuned to the emotional state of those around them. They respond not to our words, but to our nervous system — our presence, our truth, the things we carry beneath the surface. That feedback, offered without judgement and without agenda, becomes a mirror unlike any other.

Sessions at Yoga Farm are ground-based — no riding involved. The work unfolds at your pace, guided by practitioners trained in somatic and equine-assisted approaches. No experience with horses is required. They do most of the teaching.

The Offerings

Three Ways to Work with the Herd

01
Therapeutic

Equine Assisted Therapy

A psychotherapeutic modality that uses interactions with horses to support emotional growth, nervous system regulation, and healing. Sessions are guided by a trained EAT practitioner and unfold in the paddock, in the horses' world. No riding is involved — the work happens on the ground, at whatever distance feels right to you.

  • No riding or prior horse experience needed
  • Suitable for anxiety, trauma, grief, and nervous system dysregulation
  • One-on-one sessions with a trained practitioner
  • NDIS available under supported plans

NDIS: Equine Assisted Therapy at Yoga Farm is available to NDIS participants under self-managed and plan-managed plans. Get in touch to discuss how your funding can be applied.

02
Energy Healing

Reiki with the Herd

Receive a Reiki energy healing session in the presence of the herd. The horses move freely around you — they may approach, lean in, or simply hold space nearby. Each visit is unique and deeply personal. The combination of Reiki and equine presence creates a stillness that's hard to put into words until you've felt it.

  • Held outdoors in the paddock, with the herd
  • Suitable for all — no prior Reiki experience needed
  • Facilitated by Anna, Reiki Master
  • Available as private or small group

03
Private Immersion

Private Equine Assisted Therapy + Yin with Reiki

A complete layered healing session combining equine-assisted therapy, restorative Yin yoga, and Reiki in one extended private booking. For those seeking a deeper, more integrated experience — one that moves through the body at multiple levels within a single afternoon.

  • Extended private format — approximately 2.5 hours
  • Combines equine therapy, Yin yoga, and Reiki
  • Ideal after significant life transitions or for deep rest
  • Facilitated by two practitioners working together
